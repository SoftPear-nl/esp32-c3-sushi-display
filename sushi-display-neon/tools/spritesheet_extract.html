Spritesheet → packed RGB565 .bin for ESP32 / ST7789

Extracts animation strips from a sprite sheet and exports them as .bin animation files compatible with the ESP32 display project.
The background colour is auto-detected from the top-left corner and treated as transparent. Click any pixel on the sheet to pick a new background colour.

Tip: After loading, click Scan to detect all animation rows. Select one from the list, adjust Scale / FPS / Output size, then click Export .bin.

PNG, GIF, BMP — any browser-readable image.
