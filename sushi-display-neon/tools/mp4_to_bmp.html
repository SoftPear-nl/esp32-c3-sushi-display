Convert video (AVI, MP4, …) to 16-bit BMP RGB565 frames for ESP32 / ST7789

Extracts frames from a video (AVI, MP4, WebM, …) and produces 16bpp BMP BI_BITFIELDS RGB565 files — the same format used by bitmap_convert.html and the ESP32 streaming renderer.
All processing runs in the browser. No network requests. Output is a single .zip you can unpack straight into your SPIFFS flash folder.
