Convert image to 16-bit BMP RGB565 (BI_BITFIELDS)

This creates a 16bpp BMP with BI_BITFIELDS masks R=0xF800 G=0x07E0 B=0x001F, ideal for your ESP32 streaming renderer.
Output BMP is saved as top-down (negative height) to simplify reading (still valid BMP).
